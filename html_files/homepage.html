Looking for more ?

For now Diplomate is not offering any other courses. Diplomate is made by contributions of people like you, to see your branch shining here, upload the relative question papers or documents here.

Disclaimer : Diplomate do not own any of the content provided here. The actual source of question papers is BTE(Board of Technical Education) and is uploaded here by students across India.

About : Diplomate is an open-source non profit project started by students at Ambedkar Institute of Technology in 2016. The goal was to simplify the circulation of resourceful material for examinations.
